<p>The first reading,<span>&nbsp;</span><strong>Chapter 1. Introduction</strong>, provides a very detailed overview of recent advances in Data Mining. The chapter provides the definition of Data Mining, discusses motivating challenges that led to the development of the field and outlines the tasks commonly applied in mining large datasets.</p>
